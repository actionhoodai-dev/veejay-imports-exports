OUR STORY

Connecting Tradition
with Global Markets

Veejay Import & Export was founded with a singular vision: to share the rich heritage of Indian textiles and the authentic flavors of Indian spices with the world. From the intricate silk sarees of the south to the robust spices of the western Ghats, we source directly from artisans and farmers.

Our rigorous quality control and deep understanding of international trade laws have made us a trusted partner for businesses in over 59 countries.
